Privacy Policy for Icarus Manager

Last Updated: March 24, 2025

1. Introduction

Icarus Manager ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data when you use our App. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Data Collection

We use several services to provide the functionalities of the App, including:

  • Apple Sign In: For user authentication.
  • CloudKit: For storing backup data.
  • Cloudflare Namespaces: For generating and storing webhook URLs.

The only data that we intentionally collect and store are:

  • User ID: Provided via Apple Sign In.
  • Device ID: A unique identifier for your device.
  • Webhook URL: Generated via Cloudflare namespaces to properly route push notifications.

No other personal or usage data is intentionally stored on our servers.

3. Data Usage

The data we collect is used solely for the purpose of:

  • Authenticating users through Apple Sign In.
  • Generating and storing webhook URLs to route push notifications.
  • Managing encrypted backups via CloudKit.

Your data is used exclusively to ensure that notifications and app functionality work as intended. We do not use your data for marketing, advertising, or any other purposes without your consent.

4. Data Storage and Security

CloudKit: All data stored using CloudKit is encrypted by Apple. Your backups and other stored data are not kept in plaintext.

Cloudflare: Webhook URLs, User IDs, and Device IDs are securely stored in Cloudflare namespaces.

We employ industry-standard security measures to safeguard your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide the services and functionality of the App. If you decide to delete your account or uninstall the App, your User ID, Device ID, and associated Webhook URL will be removed from our systems.

6. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party services, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Apple Sign In: Used for user authentication.
  • CloudKit: Used for backup storage and secure data handling.
  • Cloudflare: Used for generating and managing webhook URLs for push notifications.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party services for additional information on how your data is handled.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on our website and within the App, and the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy will be revised accordingly. We recommend reviewing this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

  • Email: support@lanteacorp.com
  • Website: https://icarusapp.lanteacorp.com
